Make sure you are prepared:

A passion for creating solutions for business problems.

An interest in developing people skills.

A desire to mentor and coach teams.

A willingness to stay technical but go broader than the deeper.

An understanding of what is expected of software architect.

what is expected of software architect?

Make architecture decisions that guide technology decisions for teams.

Continually analyze the architecture and recommend solutions for improvement.

Analyze technology and industry trends and keep current with the latest trends.

Ensure compliance with the architecture.

Have exposure to multiple and diverse technologies, platforms, and environments.

Have certain level of domain expertise.

Possess exceptional interpersonal skills, including teamwork, facilitation, and negotiation.

Understand the political climate of the enterprise and be able to navigate to politics.
